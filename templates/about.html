About Lingual Sense

Lingual Sense is a cutting-edge application designed to detect languages from text, images, and PDF files. Powered by advanced GRU-based deep learning models, our app offers precise language predictions across a variety of input formats.

Features
  • Detect languages from plain text paragraphs.
  • Extract and identify language from images using OCR.
  • Process multi-page PDFs to extract text and predict language.
Technology Used

We leverage the power of GRU (Gated Recurrent Units), TensorFlow, Tesseract OCR, and Flask to deliver a seamless and accurate language detection experience.
